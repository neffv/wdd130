Ecuador

Ecuador is a country located in South America, known for its incredible natural diversity. It is home to the Amazon Rainforest, the Andes Mountains, and the Galápagos Islands. The country's capital, Quito, is known for its well-preserved colonial center, while Cuenca is famous for its historic architecture. Ecuador's cuisine is diverse, with dishes like ceviche and llapingachos being popular. The country's culture is a blend of indigenous and Spanish influences, making it a fascinating destination for travelers.
